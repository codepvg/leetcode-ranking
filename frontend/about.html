About CodePVG Leaderboard

Welcome to the PVG LeetCode Community! 🚀

The CodePVG LeetCode Leaderboard is designed to foster a competitive yet collaborative environment among PVG students. Our platform tracks your daily progress, celebrates your achievements, and motivates you to consistently improve your problem-solving skills.

🎯 Our Mission

To create a supportive community where PVG students can track their coding journey, learn from each other, and prepare together for technical interviews and competitive programming.

📊 Tracking System

  • Leaderboard data auto-syncs daily at 12:05 AM UTC
  • Calculates daily, weekly, and overall rankings
  • Stay up to date with fresh stats every morning

🏆 Scoring System

  • Easy Problems: 1 point
  • Medium Problems: 3 points
  • Hard Problems: 5 points

Start your journey today! Register with your LeetCode username and begin tracking your progress. Every problem solved is a step towards becoming a better programmer.
